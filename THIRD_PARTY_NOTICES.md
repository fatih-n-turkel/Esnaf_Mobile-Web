## Mobile QR rendering & scanning
- **qr_flutter** – Renders QR codes in the mobile app.
  - Package: https://pub.dev/packages/qr_flutter
  - Repository (license file): https://github.com/theyakka/qr.flutter
  - License: BSD-3-Clause

- **qr** – Dependency used by qr_flutter for QR encoding.
  - Package: https://pub.dev/packages/qr
  - Repository (license file): https://github.com/kevmoo/qr.dart
  - License: BSD-3-Clause

- **mobile_scanner** – Camera-based QR scanning in the mobile app.
  - Package: https://pub.dev/packages/mobile_scanner
  - Repository (license file): https://github.com/juliansteenbakker/mobile_scanner
  - License: BSD-3-Clause
